What the simulation is telling you

The Long Game is an educational simulation, not financial advice or a guarantee of future results. Annual returns are drawn by moving-block bootstrap from S&P 500 total returns (1928–2024), rescaled to each profile's expected return and volatility — preserving real-world fat tails and skew. LTC costs use Genworth / CareScout 2025 state medians (private room); tax and Social Security estimates use 2026 federal figures and are simplified planning approximations, not filing-grade calculations. Accounts are modeled in two buckets — tax-deferred (retirement) and taxable, assumed 70/30 of starting assets — with pre-tax contributions, tax-deferred growth taxed on withdrawal, and an annual drag on taxable gains.
